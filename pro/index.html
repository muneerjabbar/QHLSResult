About Profexel

Profexel is a unique family conference thoughtfully designed for professionals and their families. Organized by ISM Kerala, in collaboration with its professional wing Team Inspire, Profexel serves as a vibrant platform for intellectual, spiritual, and social enrichment. Learn more

Why Profexel

Profexel is more than just an event — it's a space where professional minds and family values meet. In a world filled with noise and distractions, Profexel offers a refreshing opportunity to reflect, connect, and grow — both spiritually and socially. With inspiring sessions, family-focused activities, and a vibrant professional network, Profexel is the perfect blend of purpose and togetherness.

Pre-Profexel: Coming to Your District

In the lead-up to Profexel 2.0, a series of Pre-Profexel conferences will be held across all districts of Kerala, bringing the spirit of the main event closer to you. These local gatherings aim to connect professionals, families, and communities through meaningful sessions, discussions, and a shared vision — preparing hearts and minds for the grand event in October.

Pre-Profexel conferences in India

As part of the journey towards Profexel 2.0, a series of Pre-Profexel conferences will be held in major cities across India, including Coimbatore, Mangalore, Bangalore, and more. These gatherings aim to unite professionals and their families, spark meaningful conversations, and spread the vision of Profexel to every corner of the country — building excitement and momentum for the grand event in Calicut.

Learn more

Pre-Profexel conferences in GCC

In preparation for Profexel 2.0, international Pre-Profexel conferences will be hosted in key global cities including Dubai, Doha, Muscat, and Jeddah. These events aim to connect expatriate professionals and their families with the spirit and purpose of Profexel, offering inspiring sessions, community bonding, and a preview of what’s to come in Calicut. Wherever you are, Profexel is coming closer to you!
